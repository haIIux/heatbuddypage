Privacy Policy

Updated 12/15/21

1.0 Information Collected

HeatBuddy collects basic information which includes a unique username, email address, and unique password for user accounts. For business accounts, HeatBuddy requires the aforementioned in addition to the business name, address, phone number, and current home heating oil prices. All authentication is handled by Google Firebase. All parties may request account deletion by contacting me via email support@heatbuddy.app. The information collected is not sold, solicited, or provided to any third-party marketer or advertising agency.

1.2 Location Services

HeatBuddy uses Apple's MapKit and CoreLocation technologies. HeatBuddy does not log your device location and only utilizes your device permission based on the settings you allow. Additionally, HeatBuddy only uses the location in which you enter during the instance in which you are using the application and does not continue to store or use your location for any reason. You can check your location settings by going into your System Settings -> Privacy -> Location Services and selecting HeatBuddy to determine the current setting and adjust it accordingly.

1.3 Third-Party Services

HeatBuddy uses three total third-party services which include RevenueCat, Firebase and Sublime Ads. Please note, these sites are not owned or operated by the developer of HeatBuddy therefore, HeatBuddy assumes no responsibility for their content, privacy policy, or practices. You are strongly urged to review their policies individually. You can find their respective privacy policies by using those links.

1.4 Changes of this Policy

HeatBuddy may update or change this policy as they see fit and will make every effort to alert you that this information has changed. However, the user accepts the responsibility of remaining up to date with the current Privacy Policy. At the end of the day, I am human and I am a friendly person who is not trying to steal your identity or use your information in any type of malicious fashion.

1.5 Questions? Comments?

If at any point you have any questions, comments or concerns about the application or this privacy policy please feel free to reach out to hello@heatbuddy.app and I will be happy to answer any questions.
