Features

HeatBuddy offers a native map style expierence which allows you to either use your devices current location, or search a area code or address to find the home heating oil prices in the area.


While viewing the map, users can quickly and easily see the price differences based on the color of the fuel delivery trucks on the map. If they're green, it's lower than the average price in the area and if it's red, it's higher.


HeatBuddy allows users to add businesses that may be missing and allows the user to update a price if they determine that there is a discrepency. We rely on our community members to help keep our business listings up to date when they are not immediately maintained by the businesses themselves.
