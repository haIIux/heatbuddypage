Features

HeatBuddy offers an easy to view map which allows you to use your devices current location or search an area code or address to find the cheapest home heating oil per gallon. Check out more features below!

Compare Prices

While viewing the map, you can quickly and easily see the price differences based on the color of the fuel delivery trucks on the map. If they're green, it's lower than the average price in the area and if it's red, it's higher and yellow simply means the prices are average.

Community Pricing

If you happen to determine there's a price discrepency, we allow the community to update that price accordingly to help us all ensure that we're getting the best price possible!

Your Input Matters

HeatBuddy allows users to add businesses in which they find may be missing by using a simple form. We also welcome businesses to add their business and maintain their business to ensure their current and potential customers get the most accurate information!

Save Your Favorites

Find a few businesses that you enjoy doing business with or want to quickly monitor their prices? Add them to your favorites for a quick an easy way to keep up to date, right on your home page.
