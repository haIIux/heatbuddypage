Privacy Policy

Updated 3/30/22

1.0 Information Collected

HeatBuddy collects your devices identifier for vendor (IDFV) upon launching the app. Additionally when submitting feedback from in-app, HeatBuddy collects your email address, iOS version and device model for debugging purposes. The information collected is not sold, solicited, or provided to any third-party marketer or advertising agency at any time.

1.2 Location Services

HeatBuddy uses Apple's MapKit and CoreLocation technologies. HeatBuddy does not log your device location and only utilizes your device permission based on the settings you allow. Additionally, HeatBuddy only uses the location in which you enter during the instance in which you are using the application and does not continue to store or use your location for any reason. You can check your location settings by going into your System Settings -> Privacy -> Location Services and selecting HeatBuddy to determine the current setting and adjust it accordingly.

1.3 Third-Party Services

HeatBuddy currently uses one third-party services which include Firebase. Please note, these sites are not owned or operated by the developer of HeatBuddy therefore, HeatBuddy assumes no responsibility for their content, privacy policy, or practices. You are strongly urged to review their policies individually. You can find their respective privacy policies by using those links.

1.4 Changes of this Policy

HeatBuddy may update or change this policy as they see fit and will make every effort to alert you that this information has changed. However, the user accepts the responsibility of remaining up to date with the current Privacy Policy. At the end of the day, I am human and I am a friendly person who is not trying to steal your identity or use your information in any type of malicious fashion.
